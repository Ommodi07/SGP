What is Course Crafter ?
Course Crafter is website for active-learning and finding jobs for young aspiring minds. Course Crafter is an indigenous developed platform, initiated by 23CE068, 23CE70, 23CE072 , which is instrumental for self-actualisation providing opportunities for a life-long learning.
For which standard & discipline, the Courses shall be delivered through Course Crafter?
The Course Crafter provides all the courses from UG to PG courses.
Can any person in the world register a Course on Course Crafter?
Yes, any person in the world can register a Course.
What is the eligibility criterion for joining an online course?
There is no specific eligibility / age criterion for any of the online courses. The faculty of a particular course may recommend some basic knowledge of certain topics for a person to fully grasp the contents of a course. Anyone may join an online course and register for the exam.
Is there a fee to join/register for a course?
The courses delivered through Course Crafter are available free of cost to the learners, but students who need certificate will write a proctored exam which has a nominal fee
